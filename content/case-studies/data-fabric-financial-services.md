## Challenge

The institution had accumulated over a decade of data sprawl — trading systems, risk engines, compliance databases, and customer platforms all operating in silos. Regulatory reporting required manual data collection from 12+ systems, taking days to compile.

## Approach

We implemented a comprehensive data fabric architecture:

1. **Assessment** — Catalogued 40+ source systems, mapped data flows, and identified governance gaps
2. **Lakebase** — Established a centrally governed data lake as the single source of truth
3. **Semantic Layer** — Built a unified business glossary and data catalog with automated lineage
4. **Governance** — Implemented role-based access, data quality scoring, and compliance audit trails

## Results

Regulatory reports that previously took 2-3 business days now complete in under 4 hours. The data catalog has become the go-to resource for analysts across the organisation.
