## The data problem

When we talk about "the foundations for your AI," we mean the entire stack of infrastructure that sits between your raw data sources and your intelligence layer:

- **Ingestion pipelines** that handle schema drift, late-arriving data, and format mismatches
- **a lakebase layer** that provides reliable, versioned storage with ACID guarantees
- **A data fabric** that creates a unified semantic view across disparate systems
- **Governance and lineage** so you know where every number came from

## Why this matters now

As organisations rush to adopt AI, the gap between "works in a notebook" and "works in production" is becoming painfully clear. The difference? The foundations.
